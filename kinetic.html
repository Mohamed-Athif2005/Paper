Kinetic Models

Six Pointed Spinning Top

This intricate origami project combines the classic spinning top design with a six-pointed star base. It's a visually striking piece that can be spun with a flick of the finger. The six points provide a stable base, while the carefully folded top ensures a smooth and satisfying spin. It's a challenging project for intermediate origami enthusiasts, but the rewarding outcome makes it well worth the effort.

Sliding Snake

This playful origami project creates a snake that can slither across a surface. It uses clever folds and creases to allow the snake to move forward and backward. The snake's head and tail can also be manipulated to create different poses. It's a fun and engaging project for all ages, and the finished snake can be used for imaginative play.

Flapping Bird

This classic origami project creates a bird that can flap its wings. It's a simple yet satisfying project that's perfect for beginners. The bird's wings are designed to fold and unfold, creating a realistic flapping motion. Once folded, the bird can be gently tossed into the air to watch it fly. It's a fun and engaging activity that can spark creativity and imagination.
