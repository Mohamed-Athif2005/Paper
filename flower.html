Flower Models

Rose Origami

A timeless and elegant paper craft that beautifully captures the essence of a real rose. It involves folding a square piece of paper into intricate layers to create the petals and stem, resulting in a delicate and realistic flower. Perfect for adding a touch of nature to any space, or as a thoughtful handmade gift.

Orchid Origami

A delicate and intricate flower that showcases the beauty and complexity of paper folding. It involves folding a square piece of paper into multiple layers to create the unique petal shapes and intricate details of an orchid. Perfect for those seeking a challenging and rewarding origami project.

Daisy Origami

A cheerful and simple flower that's perfect for beginners. It involves folding a square piece of paper into a few basic shapes to create the petals and center of the daisy. A great project for those who want to learn the fundamentals of origami.
